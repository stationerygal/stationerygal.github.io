STATIONERY BLOG

Hello and welcome to my stationery blog where I give you recommendations on stationery items to use to spice up your bullet journal or your everyday school/work notes!(◕ᴗ◕✿)/

Stationery Favorites of the Month

Pen

The Tombow fudenosuke is a brush pen that I’ve been using for the past month now. This pen is great for practicing calligraphy and for decorating headlines of your notes/scrapbook.

Washi Tape

This is my favorite design of the month! It’s by BGM, one of my favorite companies for washi tape. They make the tape with high quality materials and have adorable designs too.

Eraser

The Tombow mono smart eraser is a pretty nice design for small mistakes. Usually erasers are wide and difficult to just control where you erase; however with this eraser’s design you can fix your mistakes with control!
